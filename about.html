About this Website

This website is created by Kevin Buxton who has been collecting comic books for many years and wanted to find a way of displaying his comic collection on a wikipedia style website. Over the years, Kevin has collected over 400 comics, ranging from Marvel, DC, to Dark Horse Comics and many more. What is currently displayed on here is a sample of 84 comics from his collection and the information about the year the comics were released, the volume and issue numbers, as well as the names of the artists involved in making those comics, and a few comic cover art works. If you like comics you will love this website.

Happy reading, and enjoy!
